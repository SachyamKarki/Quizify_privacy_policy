Privacy Policy

Quizify Mobile Application

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to Quizify ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App") available on Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our App:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Username: When you create an account or set a username, we store your chosen username in our Firebase Realtime Database to ensure uniqueness and enable community features.
  • Profile Information: If you choose to set a profile picture, this is stored locally on your device.
  • Community Messages: Messages you send in the community chat feature, including text, images, and associated metadata (username, timestamp, country), are stored in our Firebase Realtime Database.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We collect device-specific information such as device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and device language settings.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our App, including game progress, scores, levels achieved, features used, and time spent in the App.
  • Location Data: We use IP geolocation services (ipapi.co, ip-api.com, freeipapi.com) to detect your approximate country location. This information is used to personalize your experience and is cached locally on your device for 7 days.
  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics, including screen views, feature usage, and app performance metrics.

2.3 Game Data Stored Locally

  • Game Progress: Your scores, levels achieved, game statistics, and achievements are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms.
  • Coins and Currency: Your coin balance and transaction history are stored locally on your device.
  • Settings: Your app preferences, language settings, and other configuration data are stored locally.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our App and services
  • To enable community features, including chat functionality
  • To track your game progress and achievements
  • To personalize your gaming experience
  • To analyze app performance, identify bugs, and improve user experience
  • To send push notifications (if you opt-in) using Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • To ensure username uniqueness across the platform
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Google Services

  • Firebase Analytics: We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage data. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Realtime Database: We use Firebase Realtime Database to store usernames, community chat messages, and push notification tokens. Data is stored securely and encrypted in transit. See Firebase Privacy & Security.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: We use FCM to send push notifications. Your FCM token is stored in our Firebase Database. See Firebase Privacy & Security.
  • Google Mobile Ads: Our App displays advertisements through Google Mobile Ads SDK. Google may collect information to provide personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. See Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob Privacy Policy.

4.2 IP Geolocation Services

We use third-party IP geolocation services (ipapi.co, ip-api.com, freeipapi.com) to detect your approximate country location. These services may collect your IP address. Please review their privacy policies:

  • ipapi.co Privacy Policy
  • ip-api.com Legal
  • freeipapi.com Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Most of your game data, including scores, progress, coins, and settings, is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system.

Cloud Storage: The following data is stored in our Firebase Realtime Database:

  • Usernames (for uniqueness checking)
  • Community chat messages (including text, images, usernames, timestamps, and country information)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens (for push notifications)

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Data stored locally on your device remains until you uninstall the App or clear app data.
  • Cloud Data: Usernames and chat messages are retained in our Firebase Database. Chat messages are limited to the last 100 messages. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.
  • Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics data is retained according to Google's data retention policies (typically 14 months).
  • IP Geolocation Cache: Your country location is cached locally for 7 days before being refreshed.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can access your personal data stored in the App through the App's settings and features.
  • Deletion: You can delete your local data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data through your device settings. To request deletion of cloud-stored data (username, chat messages), please contact us.
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: You can opt out of Firebase Analytics data collection through your device settings or by contacting us.
  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's advertising settings.
  • Push Notifications: You can disable push notifications through your device settings.
  • Community Chat: You can choose not to use the community chat feature. Messages you send are publicly visible to other users.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area) without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using our App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries, including the United States where our Firebase services are hosted.

  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.
